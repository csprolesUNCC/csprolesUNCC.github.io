In less than a week from the time of writing this post, I will be walking across the stage in Halton Arena, receiving my Bachelors degree in Computer Science.

This post is just an introduction and me sharing my plans going forward.

My plan for this site.

This site is intended to allow me a way to exercise the skills I learned in college. It is a personal project, which will likely only be viewed by people I provide the link to. Still, I will make an effort to stay active here.

"The best way to learn is by doing."

What will I post about?

I will be posting about whatever comes to mind. That could be my thoughts on a Hurricanes game, my opinion on a Gamecocks coaching hire, speculations about upcoming video games, or even just a post about my dog.

When will I post?

My goal is to post at least once every two weeks. I may not always hit that mark, but I will try my best to stay consistent.

I hope to see some support during this new journey, and hope to expand this site beyond what is already implemented.
