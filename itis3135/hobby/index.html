About Me

My fascination and passion for the outdoors and hiking specifically began when I was young, and I joined the Boy Scouts. It was thorough this that I was able to experience nature and hiking in so many different places, and the love for doing so has stuck with me.

Welcome to my Hiking Hobby Page

Hiking is the perfect blend of exercise and adventure, letting you explore the beauty of nature one step at a time, and giving you a chance to really disconnect from the hectic aspects of modern life.

Best Times to Hike

Spring and Fall are my favorite times to hike, with cooler weather and beautiful foliage. Still, the Summer months provide lush greenery and a variety of wildlife, while Winter months give way to bare trees and snowy paths.

Best Hiking Locations

Personally, I'm not too picky about where I hike. However, there are some places I have been that I find myself thinking about for days after.

Some of my favorite locations include:

  • Blue Ridge Mountains, North Carolina
  • Rocky Mountains, Colorado
  • Carson National Forest, New Mexico

How to Get Started

Hiking has a wide range of difficulties.

Some trails only require a bottle of water for a fun day. Others might require a heavy pack, ice spikes for your shoes, or even camping equipment.

I recommend anyone new to hiking to start out with an easy one. There are plenty of light trails around the country, and plenty of resources such as AllTrails that allow users to read about the difficulty of these trails. As you hike, you can slowly work up to harder trails, and eventually get to the point of backpacking on long treks across multiple days!
